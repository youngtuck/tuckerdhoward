The project

Young Tuck is the music project of Tucker Dane Howard — songwriter, producer, and performer operating at the intersection of indie pop, alternative R&B, and conceptual world-building. Independent since 2017.

The catalog spans singles, EPs, and fully produced projects — each built as a complete creative world with its own visual identity, sonic palette, and emotional arc. Everything is written, produced, and directed in-house.

45M+ global streams. International collaborations. Live performances building a real community around the music — not just numbers.
