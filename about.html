Tucker Dane Howard is a visual designer, AI creative, and musician whose work exists at the edge of disciplines. Born from a background spanning fine art, technology, and music production, his practice bridges the gap between how things look and how they feel.

Currently based in Pasadena, CA. Previously at Meta as an AI Model Designer, developing structured prompt systems for global advertising production at scale. Work has been exhibited at MOXI — The Wolf Museum of Exploration + Innovation in Santa Barbara.

As Young Tuck, he's accumulated 45M+ global streams across an independent music catalog that blends conceptual world-building with sound design.
