Chapter I The
Origin
5 Years in the Making

A benchmark that once felt distant.
Soul Ties closed the gap.

Since the release of Koi's track "Sbs" in 2019, his work — both musically and visually — represented a benchmark for what independent artists could achieve. His videos, particularly those produced by the now-defunct studio Overcast, were known for their cinematic polish and high-budget feel.

In the summer of 2024, living with a highly skilled videographer created the ideal conditions for experimentation at scale. The goal expanded: if a collaboration with Koi were possible, the result should be definitive — not just a video, but a centerpiece.

Chapter II Visual
Concept
Duality

Two sonic phases.
Two visual worlds.

After extensive research across Los Angeles venues, a location was discovered that embodied the concept perfectly: a split environment where one half of the space was light blue and the other pink, each furnished with its own couch. The physical dichotomy became the narrative framework.

First half Blue world

Cool environment. One wardrobe. The opening sonic phase — introspective, atmospheric, grounded in stillness.

Second half Pink world

Warm environment. Alternate styling. The beat switch fires — energy shifts, palette shifts, world shifts.

Chapter IV The
Moment
Influence Becoming Dialogue

Less like an encounter with a celebrity.
More like a creative exchange between peers.

Meeting and working directly with an artist who had shaped one's own sound can be disorienting. The dynamic shifts from distant admiration to immediate collaboration — from fan to co-author.

Despite moments of excitement and disbelief, the atmosphere felt natural. The experience suggested a new possibility: influence does not have to remain aspirational. It can become collaborative.

Working through the night on the rough cut, it became clear the final product had achieved — and in some respects exceeded — the visual quality that had once seemed unattainable. The comparison to Overcast's output was no longer hypothetical. The standard had been matched on independent terms.

"Influence does not have to remain aspirational. It can become collaborative."

Soul Ties · Young Tuck ft. Koi · 2024
Legacy

A proof-of-capability.
Aspiration transformed into precedent.

Soul Ties represents a convergence of long-term artistic influence, technical production growth, expanding professional network, and increased logistical capability. As a standalone single, it occupies a unique place in the catalog — not part of a larger album narrative, yet carrying the weight of a milestone event.

It demonstrates that independent artists can produce work with cinematic scope when vision, collaboration, and determination align.
