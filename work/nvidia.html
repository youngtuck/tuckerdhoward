Chapter 01

The Question

What does AI-powered hardware actually look like when the design is as intelligent as the product?

The RTX 60 Series launch presented a fundamental visual problem: how do you communicate the invisible? Neural processing, AI acceleration, real-time intelligence — these are concepts that exist beneath the surface of the hardware. The visual system needed to make that inner intelligence visible.

"Neural light in physical space" — the visual motif that guided every decision in this system, from lighting to layout to social format.

Core creative direction · Visual System Study

This is an independent concept study — not a commissioned NVIDIA project. It was built to explore how a designer with deep AI-integration experience would approach a next-generation GPU launch at the scale and ambition level NVIDIA actually operates at.

Chapter 02

Visual Language System

Color · Typography · Lighting — three systems working in unison.

A strong visual system isn't a mood board — it's a set of decisions that can be applied consistently by any designer on any surface. For RTX 60, three interlocking systems were defined before a single asset was produced.

Color System Dark matter + neural glow

Typography Neue Haas Grotesque

Lighting System 3-layer approach

Hard rim light for precision. Volumetric glow for intelligence. Directional light flow for implied motion and scale.
